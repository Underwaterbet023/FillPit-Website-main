About Us

In our project focused on fixing potholes, we are developing a user-friendly website where citizens can actively participate in reporting potholes across India. Users can upload images of the potholes along with the live location, making it easier for the government to track and address these issues. The data collected is categorized state-wise, enabling the authorities to identify problematic areas and prioritize road repairs. This initiative aims to improve road safety, reduce vehicle damage, and ensure smoother commuting experiences for all citizens by streamlining the process of reporting and fixing potholes in a timely manner.
