Road Repair Project

This project was undertaken by the FillPit team to address a severe pothole issue that had been causing traffic disruptions and vehicle damage for several months. The road had deteriorated due to heavy rainfall and inadequate drainage systems.

Our team conducted a thorough assessment of the damage and developed a comprehensive repair plan. We began by removing the damaged asphalt, properly preparing the base, and then applying a high-quality asphalt mix designed to withstand local weather conditions and traffic loads.

The repair work was completed in just two days, minimizing inconvenience to local residents and commuters. We also improved the drainage system to prevent similar issues in the future.

Rajiv Chowk, Delhi
Completed on: March 15, 2025
